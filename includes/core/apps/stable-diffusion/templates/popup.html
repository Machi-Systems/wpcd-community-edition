Instructions and FAQS

How do I use my server?

Navigate to your servers page. In the REQUEST IMAGES panel enter an image description. Then, click the green REQUEST IMAGES button. It will take between one or two minutes to generate your image (if it's the first in the queue.)

How To Retrieve Your Images

After requesting an image, the request is placed into a queue. If it's the first one, it will take 1 - 2 minutes. Refresh your page then and links to the generated images will appear at the bottom of the REQUEST IMAGES panel. Thumbnails of the most recent generated images will also appear in the GRID panels.

Requesting Multiple Images

You can make as many requests for images as you like - it's your server afterall. But, images are generated one at a time. Your requests are placed into a queue and processed first-in-first-out. You can see the number of requests pending at the top of the server panel.

Image Link Expiration

Links to all images expire approximately 7 days after you generate them. In certain situations we might be able to recover images after 7 days if you send us the original link. Please open a support ticket to make this request.
